Het Diner

Het Diner is a exhibiation that took place on Oktober 8th 2019 in the studio Graphic Design of Luca school of arts. As a part of the series Cabinet of Contents. Three third year students; Marijke Aeissing, Silke Geerts and Elien Laenen had the honour to host the first exhibtion. Starting from the love of hosting (dinner)parties, they had the idea to create one festive stillife within the cabinets. The result is a chaos caused by the clashing of different tableware. This was combined with printed out scans of other tabelware, which in turn caused an interesting effect of 2D and 3D. By putting all the cabinets in a line and adding a white table cloth "het diner" (the dinner) was formed.

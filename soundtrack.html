The Cook, The Thief, His Wife and Her Lover

An album cover inspired by the Peter Greenaway movie "The Cook, The Thief, His Wife and Her Lover. The movie mostly takes place in a restaurant and is shot in 4 different colours depending on the character. I tried to visualise this by making a still live in these colours, with objects that fit the characters.
